I am always interested in working with self-motivated students on challening research problems. Before reaching out to me, kindly take the time to read this post. I will provide some general advice that is applicable to all students, followed by specific advice tailored to different categories of students.

For all students

When getting in touch with me, kindly provide a comprehensive curriculum vitae (CV) that outlines your educational achievements, any previous research experience you may have had, references to any code you have authored, and a list of software programs with which you are proficient.

Please take the opportunity to review my publications and the suggested reading list here. Additionally, include a detailed explanation of how your interests and background align with my research endeavors.
